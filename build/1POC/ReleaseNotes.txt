# Mega Awesome Mining Robot Game POC

- Cool Robot (that's you)
You are the robot. You run on electricity.
- Mr. Bad Guy Spider Enemies
They are bad guys. They run on being bad.
- Robust Item System
Collect some ores with your laser.
- Battery Charging
You run on batteries. Step on the charging pad or you'll be really slow and can't shoot.
- Mining Asteroid
You are on an asteroid. You have a laser. You shoot the asteroid. You get ores.
